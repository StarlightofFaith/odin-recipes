Cinnamon Sugar Snack Cakes

Description

While this cake is delicious any time of day, its warm spices and tangy frosting lend themselves particularly well to breakfast or brunch, pairing perfectly with a cup of coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, plus more for garnish, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons melted unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup plus 1/3 cup white sugar, divided
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 6 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 2/3 cup whipping cream, chilled

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and position rack in center of oven
  2. Coat a 9-inch square baking pan with cooking spray
  3. Line bottom of pan with parchment paper and coat paper with cooking spray
  4. Whisk together flour, baking powder, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, and salt in a medium bowl
  5. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted w/ whisk attachment, mix together oil and butter
  6. Mix in 1/2 cup white sugar and brown sugar until well combined
  7. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well to incorporate and scraping bowl well after each addition
  8. Mix in 2 teaspoons vanilla
  9. Add about 1/3 of flour mixture; mix at low speed until combined
  10. Mix in half of buttermilk, scraping bowl well
  11. Repeat, alternating between flour and buttermilk until both are fully incorporated and batter is smooth
  12. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread in an even layer
  13. Stir together remaining 1/3 cup white sugar and 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon in a small bowl
  14. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar evenly over batter
  15. Wash and dry stand mixer bowl and whisk attachment
  16. Bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean or w/ few moist crumbs (20-25 minutes)
  17. Transfer pan to wire rack and let cool completely
  18. For frosting, in bowl of stand mixer, beat cream cheese at medium speed 1 minute
  19. Add powdered sugar and beat at medium speed until well combined, scrape bowl well
  20. With mixer running at medium-low speed, add cream and remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla in a slow, steady stream
  21. Gradually increase speed to medium-high and beat to medium peaks (2-3 minutes more)
  22. Fit a disposable pastry bag with a large round or star tip (about 1/4-inch wide) and fill with frosting
  23. Run small offset spatula or knife around outside of cake and gently invert onto cutting board
  24. Remove parchment paper and cut into 9 squares
  25. Pipe frosting onto each cake piece (start at one corner of square, pipe zigzag diagonally, ending at opposite corner)
  26. Garnish with cinnamon
  27. Chill in airtight container until ready to serve (up til 4 days)
