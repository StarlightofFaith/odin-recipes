Grilled Peaches and Cream

Description

This is an easy grilled peach dessert! I use a honey nut flavored cream cheese spread and it's wonderful! Drizzle a little extra honey after they're grilled and they're perfect!

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons clover honey
  • 1 cup soft cream cheese with honey and nuts
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  2. Brush peaches with light coating of oil
  3. Place pit side down onto the grill, and grill for five minutes or until surface has nice grill marks
  4. Turn peaches over and drizzle with a little honey
  5. Place dollop of cream cheese where peach pit was
  6. Grll to 2-3 more minutes or until filling is warm
  7. Serve immediately
