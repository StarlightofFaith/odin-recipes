Peach and Blackberry Cobbler

Description

This sweet, cakey cobbler with fresh blackberries and juicy peaches is perfect for any summer occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups peeled and sliced fresh peaches
  • 1 cup fresh blackberries
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix peaches, blackberries, 1/4 cup sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice in bowl
  3. Pour butter into 9x13-inch glass baking dish and evenly coat the bottom and sides
  4. Whisk flour, 1 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl; mix in milk, stirring just to moisten dry ingredients
  5. Pour batter into prepared baking dish and distribute fruit mixture evenly over batter
  6. Bake in preheated oven until cobbler is golden brown (about 45 minutes)
